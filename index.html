Background

Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is a leading cause of mortality globally, particularly in India. Limited access to specialized healthcare facilities, especially in rural areas, delays diagnosis and treatment. Our mobile application offers an innovative solution by leveraging mobile health (mHealth) technology, enabling users to take pictures of ECG reports for cloud-based analysis using advanced machine learning algorithms. This makes CVD screening more accessible to the general population.
